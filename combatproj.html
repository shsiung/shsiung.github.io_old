Combat Robot Club

Combat robot club is a student-run club that basically builds a massive robot that combats with other robots in in Robogames 2013. The Robogames is, unfortunately, discontinued, the competition website is here. I participated in several machining jobs as well as inputting ideas in terms of weapon and overall design ideas. Below are some CAD pictures of our robot design.

The right picture shows the overall mass distribution of the robot. As regulated by the competition, the robot can only be at most 200lbs. The left picture shows the over all design of our robot. The weapon system of the robot consists of a spinning top with three arms made from aircraft-graded aluminum (7075 alloy). At the end of the aluminum arms, there are sharp blades made from heat treated T6 tool steel. From our calculation, the spinning speed could reach at most 5000-7000 RPM depending on the battery.
