E11 Mudduino(Arduino) Robot

E11-Autonomous Vehicles is an introductory class offered for first-year undergraduate student at Harvey Mudd College. The class is a hands-on interdisciplinary introduction to mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering. I took it during the fall of 2012. I was part of a team of two. Below are the CAD designs of the two robots that we used for the final competition of the class.

The competition was held on a rectangular arena. On the arena, there are 9 photo gates which emit specific gold codes. Each gate has a team color indicating which team possesses it currently. The goal of the competition is to use the robot to "tag" those photo gates. At the end of the competition, the number of gates possessed by each team is counted, and the team with the most points win the round (each round is 2 minutes).
